# Live Flight Tracker - Version Roadmap

================================================
V2 - Enhanced Flight Data
================================================
- Departure airport
- Destination airport
- Aircraft home country
- Distance to destination airport
- Time already in flight
- Estimated time remaining to destination
- Status flags: "About to take off" / "Just landed"

================================================
V3 - Web & Intelligence
================================================
- Web-based launch with interactive UI/UX
- AI chat for questions about current flight data
- Departure and destination aerodrome weather
- Current aerodrome weather conditions
- Aircraft information and flight limits
- Continuous monitoring (auto-refresh every 5 minutes)

================================================
V4 - Advanced Analytics & Prediction
================================================
- Full plane trip tracker
- Flight tickets and schedules integration
- Mission feasibility checker using METAR and TAF data
- Algorithm to predict flight delays based on weather
  vs scheduled departure time
- Holding pattern predictor based on airport traffic
  and weather conditions at destination
